Giuseppe Peano

The great Italian mathematician

Life of Giuseppe Peano:

  • Born in 1858 in Spineta, Kingdom of Sardinia (modern Italy)
  • graduated the university of Turin in 1880
  • published his first book, a textbook on calculus in 1884
  • published the "Peano Axioms" in 1889
  • started the formulario project in 1891
  • published the final edition of the formulario project in 1908
  • suffered a heart attack and died in 1932 in Turin, Italy
